About NYC Eviction Data

This website presents data on eviction cases across New York City. The dataset includes details such as eviction addresses, dates, boroughs, and more, sourced from NYC Open Data.

Why It Matters

The eviction data is important to understand housing stability, trends in possession cases, and impacts on communities throughout NYC.

Explore Eviction Cases

Explore individual eviction cases using filters, or visit the Analysis page for charts and summaries that highlight trends in the eviction data.
